    • Basketball training for Beginners

    Beginner programs focus on teaching kids how to play the game the right way while encouraging creative freedom in a positive, fun-filled and energetic atmosphere.

    Critical skill development areas include body posture, ball control, ball handling, and footwork.

    Learning the importance of these elements from an early age will allow for an easier grasp on fundamentals of the game such as: shooting, passing, and dribbling.

    Individual as well as team concepts will be taught to encourage collaboration among students.

    Age appropriate equipment will be used.

    Girls Basketball Programs

    Our girl's training academy has a curriculum similar to that of our elite training programs. Past experience is beneficial to successfully complete the program but is not a mandatory requirement.

    An emphasis will be placed on teaching proper body mechanics, basketball skills, and progressively executing drills at game speed.

    Appropriately sized equipment will be used.
